History

History describes miracles and events of the past. Kamala Tannery is currently creating tomorrow’s history.

During early 1990's Kamala Tannery made a modest beginning as a boutique sub contract manufacturing facility with minimum labour force and machinery.

From Mid to late 90's, it was developed to become a full-fledged Goat leather tannery by adding a new wet unit and finishing machinery to start catering to the domestic markets.

In 2001, it made big strides forward, to enter into the first joint venture in the Indian Leather industry - with a name no less than “Conceria Antiba“ which is a fashion tannery in Italy and has an established presence across the globe since 1987.

Vision

We believe that we are catering the best quality finished leather and we are constantly focusing on innovation. We will engage in sustainable practices and anticipate the needs of our customers globally with a focus on environmental stewardship, activities to benefit society. We believe that we need to own and control the primary technologies behind the products that we make.

Mission

To become the premier manufacturing organization in the leather industry, gaining market competitiveness and diversifying into value added leather with clean, well maintained and friendly environment and to become the No.1 in the Finished Leather Segment category in India.

Future Plans

Already the blue print is ready for adding a patent plant to the tannery. Prime International is set to emerge a leader again with the production of soft patent leathers in this plant.

Why Choose Us

Soaking

First step to rehydrate the Leather to make it suitable for tanning process.

Tanning

A process by which the leather is made ready for commercial use and improves preservation of leather.

Shaving

Shaving, fine tunes the unevenness of the leather to the required thickness as per the final product of leather.

Dying

Dyeing gives major properties to leather needed for final product, such as softness, flatness, Color etc.

Toggling

Toggling flattens and dries the leather to normal room moisture content, making it suitable for finishing process.

Buffing

Buffing operation scrubs off the uneven defects of the leather and get the leather surface ready for finishing.

Finishing

Finishing, gives character to the leather, such as Feel, Shine, Color, water-repellency and appearance.

Inspection & Measuring

physical and chemical tests carried out to international standards and customer specifications. Measures the area of the leather to decimal accuracy.
