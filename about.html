About Us

Our History

History describes miracles and events of the past. Kamala Tannery is currently creating tomorrow’s history.

During early 1990's Kamala Tannery made a modest beginning as a boutique sub contract manufacturing facility with minimum labour force and machinery.

From Mid to late 90's, it was developed to become a full-fledged Goat leather tannery by adding a new wet unit and finishing machinery to start catering to the domestic markets.

In 2001, it made big strides forward, to enter into the first joint venture in the Indian Leather industry - with a name no less than “Conceria Antiba“ which is a fashion tannery in Italy and has an established presence across the globe since 1987.

Social Responsibility

Kamala Tannery Ltd. have primary Effluent Treatment Plants in all the units as per the guidelines set by UP Pollution Control Broad & Ministry of Environment & Forests India.

All the Plants connected to common secondary treatment plant where as all units has in house chrome recovery and primary treatment plant. Additionally, we also promote "Eco Friendly" articles, which are free from Azo Dyes, PCP and Chrome 6.

We also implementing the Social Audit in every manufacturing unit of our company. We always try our best efforts to create awareness about environmental conservation among inhabitants of towns/cities where our units are located by various programmes, medical camps and educational shows.

Environment

Care for the environment and protection has been a major concern of the company from the date of its establishment, In creating leather from the hide & skin, by-products of the world meat industries, Everest Tannery enhances the beauty & performance of a natural renewable material.

The company has always taken a responsible attitude to the environment and has adoped and changed manufacturing process to take into account the health and safety of its employees and the impact of its operations on the environment.

We aim to minimise waste through the stringent use of all materials, supplies and energy, and use renewable or recyclable materials and components wherever possible in operations.

Our factory is connected to the common secondary treatment plant, and our unit has in-house primary treatment plant and chrome recovery plant. Our belt leather production is vegetable tanned leather, a genuine and ecological leather obtained with natural procedures, unharmful to the environment.
